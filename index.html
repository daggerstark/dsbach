El Yunque Rainforest

Self-guided exploration of the only tropical rainforest in the U.S. National Forest System.

Stop 1: Juan Diego Falls & Swimming Hole (1 Hour)

The Vibe: Stunning waterfall pool perfect for a dip.

What to do: A quick 5-min walk takes you to the lower falls. It's a steep, muddy scramble, but leads to a secluded 40-foot waterfall! (Note: Wear water shoes or grippy hiking boots!)

Stop 2: Mt. Britton Tower Hike (1.5 - 2 Hours)

The Vibe: A short, rewarding hike with serious elevation and 360-degree views.

What to do: Follow the paved (but slippery) trail uphill through the lush forest. Less than a mile, but a 600ft climb. Climb the stone tower for dramatic misty views of the mountains and ocean!
